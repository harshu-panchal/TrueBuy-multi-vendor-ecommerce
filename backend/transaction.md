How to use:
Vendors: Go to Earnings -> Withdrawals tab to request a payout.
Delivery Boys: Click on the Earnings card on your dashboard to see your balance and request money.
Admins: Navigate to Analytics & Finance -> Money Requests to process pending payouts.
The system ensures financial integrity by "locking" funds once a request is approved, preventing double-spending, and maintaining a clear audit trail for all payouts.
